Skärgårdens Montessori

Education

Skärgårdens Montessori pre-school had an aging, inaccessible website and problems managing its mailing lists and other digital services.

I designed a series of digital solutions to increase member involvement and reduce staff workload, and I drafted a digital strategy for the school’s future communications. The first two phases of this ongoing project introduced a responsive site and an upgraded newsletter function.

TOOLS
Sketch, Adobe CS, WordPress, custom Genesis child theme, Advanced Custom Fields, MailPoet integration.

Unlikely Objects

Studio

For my thesis work at Hyper Island I founded Unlikely Objects, a storytelling company that develops digital games and books. This company was created in coordination with Blekinge Business Incubator’s Startup Academy.

Unlikely Objects began with a simple landing page but soon required a layout that could accommodate a series of diverse projects. The new design allows for easy individual project promotion through custom landing pages and mailing list segments.

TOOLS
Sketch, Adobe CS, WordPress, custom Genesis child theme, Advanced Custom Fields, MailChimp integration.

The Invisible D & Wednesday

Book projects

2018 saw the creation of two book projects: The Invisible D and Wednesday the Raven. Both are ongoing projects created in the public space: The Invisible D was first published as a daily images during Suicide Awareness month, and Wednesday the Raven is (initially) being published a chapter per week, with all proofreading and editing tracked through Github. Additional teaching materials are being developed for The Invisible D, and both projects will go to print in 2019.

TOOLS
The Invisible D – Adobe CS
Wednesday the Raven – Ulysses, Jekyll static site, Github

Move Management

Web app

After studying leadership and team development at Hyper Island I was eager to work with Move Management, a company that focuses on facilitation, coaching and teaching people to build stronger teams.

Part of their digitalization strategy involves a new tool to measure team quality. This web app had several unusual requirements for displaying data and required multiple prototypes in Sketch, Hype and Invision.

TOOLS
Sketch, Tumult Hype, Invision.
